About Us

Trusted by Parents, Loved by Kids

At Franklite, we believe childhood should be full of fun, movement, and adventure. That’s why we design high-quality, safe, and colorful ride-on toys like Bazuka, City Bike, and Minni Boomer — built to spark imagination and endless smiles.

With a focus on safety, comfort, and playful design, Franklite creates ride-on toys that kids love and parents trust. Our mission is to make every ride a happy memory — one bike, boomer, or trike at a time.

Built for Adventure

Our ride-on toys like Bazuka and City Bike are designed for action-packed fun, keeping kids active and full of excitement every day.

Bright & Colorful Designs

Each toy bursts with vibrant colors and playful styling to capture young imaginations and bring joy to every ride.

Safety Comes First
